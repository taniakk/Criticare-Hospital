Dr. Sundeep G. Mestri

General Physician, pulmonologist, Critical Care Specialist

MD (Chest Medicine And Tuberculosis - Mumbai University) from B.Y.L.C. Nair Hospital, Mumbai. MBBS (Bombay University) from L.T.M.M.C. - Sion Hospital, Mumbai.

Dr. Sundeep Mestri is a practicing General Physician/ Pulmonologist for 22 years in Navi Mumbai with special instrest in Treating -:

Fever (All Kinds), Diabetic, Hypertension, Diabetes mellitus, TB/ bronchial asthma/ copd, Covid Related Lung Illness, Chronic Cough, Critically Ill Patient, Ventilator Related Patients, Pneumonia

He has been heading consultant at

  • Criticare Hospital ICCU, Multi-speciality & Trauma centre
  • MGM New Bombay Hospital Vashi
  • Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital, Navi Mumbai (formerly Reliance Hospital)
  • Vinamra Swaraj Hospital Vashi
  • National Burns Hospital

MISSION:

  • To try and prevent lung diseases with effective control of risk factors.
  • To spread knowledge to patients and family physicians in early diagnosis and treatment of lung diseases.
  • To provide comprehensive and compassionate care to patients suffering from all types of pulmonary diseases.
  • To provide awareness regarding:
  • Pulmonary tuberculosis / MDR – XDR TB
  • Obstructive Sleep Apnea
  • Covid and Post Covid Lung issues
  • Covid and Post Covid Lung issues
  • COPD / Asthma
  • Pneumonia
  • ILD – Interstitial Lung Disease
  • Occupational Lung Disease

VISION:

  • To raise awareness regarding lung health.
  • To create, disseminate and apply new knowledge to reduce the burden of respiratory disease.

OBJECTIVE:

  • To provide exceptional patient care
  • Develop innovative ways to improve patient care
  • Spread the use of best practices for lung care
  • To engage patients in their own health improvement
