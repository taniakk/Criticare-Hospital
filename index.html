ABOUT CRITICARE HOSPITAL

Excellence in Critical Care & Multi-Speciality Healthcare

Criticare ICCU Multispeciality & Trauma Centre is one of the most trusted and reputed hospitals in Airoli, providing comprehensive healthcare services under one roof at an affordable cost. Conveniently located for patients from Thane, Navi Mumbai and surrounding regions, the hospital is equipped with modern medical infrastructure and advanced treatment facilities.

With a strong commitment to patient safety, hygiene, and quality healthcare, our experienced consultants, nursing staff, paramedics and support teams work together to deliver compassionate and personalized treatment for every patient.

Our facilities include 24×7 Trauma & Emergency Care, a fully equipped ICU, advanced diagnostics, Digital X-Ray, ECG and comprehensive healthcare support services designed to meet the highest standards of medical excellence.
