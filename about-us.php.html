About Hospital

Criticare Hospital ICCU, Multi-speciality & Trauma centre

Criticare ICCU Multispeciality & Trauma Centre is one of the most trusted & reputed hospital in Airoli, that provides you with One stop all facilities under one roof at an affordable cost. It is easily accessible from thane and Vashi and has all the modern facilities. It caters to about a population of around 100,000 and is well-equipped centre spread over an area of 5000sq feet.

The hospital maintains utmost hygiene, ensuring safety and quality service to the patients. The team at the Hospital is friendly and caring, the Consultants, Staff Nurses, Aaya, Maushi, and Paramedics all strive hard to fulfill the requirement of the patients and arrange all facilities under one roof.

Criticare ICCU Multispeciality & Trauma Centre provides affordable and quality healthcare. Offered by the team of compassionate and dedicated medical professionals, with 24 X 7 Trauma, Emergency Care, 35 bedded ICU with modern life saving equipment’s, Advanced Diagnostics, Digital X-ray, ECG, Lab (outsource) Services
